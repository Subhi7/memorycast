The Problem
One model doesn't fit all time-series

High-volatility revenue data needs ML. Stable seasonal demand needs ETS. There's no single best forecasting model — and humans can't reason across thousands of past runs to know which to pick.

The Solution
An agent that learns its own rules

MemoryCast profiles each series, queries Cognee for similar past runs, selects models autonomously, and writes new rules into its own SKILL.md — permanently improving its strategy.
